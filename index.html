How I work

My process

I approach design as a system, not a moment - combining research, strategy, and iteration to shape experiences that are both purposeful and consistent.

01

Discover

I start by building deep context - understanding users, business goals, market dynamics, and brand positioning. Through research, audits, and conversations, I uncover the real problem behind the brief.

02

Define

Insights are shaped into direction. I synthesise research into clear problem framing, user needs, and strategic principles that guide both the experience and how the brand shows up within it.

03

Design

I translate strategy into experience - exploring concepts through sketching, prototyping, and high-fidelity design. Every decision considers usability, clarity, and how the product expresses the brand.

04

Refine

Designs are tested, challenged, and evolved. Through usability feedback and iteration, I refine solutions until they work seamlessly in real-world use and remain consistent with the intended experience and brand direction.
